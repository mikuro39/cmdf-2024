Data Science Interview Prep Timeline

  • Week 1: March 10, 2024

    Introduction to Data Science and Python Basics. Learn the basics of data science and Python programming, focusing on Python syntax, data types, and basic operations.

  • Week 2: March 17, 2024

    Data Manipulation with Pandas. Explore Pandas for data manipulation and analysis. Focus on data cleaning, transformation, and aggregation techniques.

  • Week 3: March 24, 2024

    Data Visualization. Begin exploring data visualization using Matplotlib and Seaborn. Learn to communicate results effectively through visual representation.

  • Week 4: March 31, 2024

    Statistical Analysis. Study the fundamentals of statistical analysis. Focus on concepts like probability distributions, hypothesis testing, and statistical significance.

  • Week 5: April 7, 2024

    Introduction to Machine Learning. Cover basic machine learning concepts, focusing on supervised vs. unsupervised learning and simple algorithms like linear regression.

  • Week 6: April 14, 2024

    Machine Learning - Classification. Explore classification algorithms in machine learning, including logistic regression, decision trees, and SVMs.

  • Week 7: April 21, 2024

    SQL for Data Science. Learn SQL essentials for data science. Practice querying databases to extract and manipulate data effectively for analysis.

  • Week 8: April 28, 2024

    Advanced Machine Learning Concepts. Dive into more advanced machine learning topics, including ensemble methods, k-nearest neighbors (KNN), and an introduction to neural networks.

  • Week 9: May 5, 2024

    Real-World Data Science Projects. Start applying what you've learned to real-world data science projects. This could involve participating in Kaggle competitions or working on personal projects that interest you.

  • Week 10: May 12, 2024

    Review and Practice Problems. Consolidate your knowledge and skills by revisiting key concepts and tackling various data science practice problems.
